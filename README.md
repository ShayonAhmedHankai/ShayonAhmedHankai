## 👋 About Me

I'm **Shayon Ahmed Hankai**, a Discord Manager and Developer from **Gazipur, Bangladesh**, working with **Akhilur Rahman Tutul (AiVEN)** at **Levelyn Esports** to build the esports ecosystem in Bangladesh.

My focus is on **community architecture**, **backend development**, and **digital content creation**. I specialize in Discord automation, Python scripting, and exploring cybersecurity through ethical hacking. Beyond code, I create esports content on YouTube and push creative boundaries on TikTok.

I believe in **evolving through data** and **securing the digital frontier**—building systems that are purposeful, scalable, and real.
